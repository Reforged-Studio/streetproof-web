StreetProof is designed with privacy as a default. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Summary

StreetProof does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or store your personal data on any server. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. All data stays on your device or in Apple Health.

Data the app uses

Location data

StreetProof records your GPS location during active workouts to draw your route on the map and build your coverage map over time. Location is captured to record your route during active workouts and to center the map on your current position; the app does not access your location at other times. The app uses "When In Use" location authorization. Location data is stored on your device and in Apple Health (as part of the workout's route data); it is not transmitted to any server.

Health and fitness data

With your permission, StreetProof reads your past workouts and GPS routes from Apple Health so they appear on your coverage map and in workout history. During active workouts, the app saves the completed workout, route, and metrics (such as heart rate, distance, pace, and running form metrics from Apple Watch) to Apple Health so they appear in the Fitness app. All Health data stays on your device unless you have enabled iCloud sync for Apple Health, which is managed by Apple, not by StreetProof.

Reminder pins

Pins you create in the app (to mark stops, missed blocks, or other notes) are stored as files on your device. They are not transmitted anywhere.

Data the app does not collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or contact information
  • No advertising identifiers or device identifiers used for tracking
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reporting through third-party services
  • No data shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party

Apple services used

StreetProof uses the following Apple frameworks, which are governed by Apple's privacy policy:

HealthKit
Reading and writing workouts and routes
MapKit
Displaying maps
WeatherKit
Current weather conditions during workouts
Core Location
GPS recording

Apple's privacy policy applies to data handled by these frameworks. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's privacy

StreetProof is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
